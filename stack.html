Technology Philosophy

My approach to choosing and using technologies.

Stability Over Trend

I prefer battle-tested technologies with strong community support over the latest hype.

Interoperability

Technologies should work well together and not lock you into a specific ecosystem.

Documentation

Clear documentation is non-negotiable—both for the tech I choose and what I build.

Community

Active community support means better troubleshooting and long-term viability.
